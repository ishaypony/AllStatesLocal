Some of the common issues with your air conditioner system

Warm or Insufficient Air

Sometimes there are no vents open in a home that make it difficult to cool. Closed vents cause issues for air conditioners to keep up and make the house warm. A warm house shows that your house's air conditioner is too small to make cooling for you. The clogged air filter in your air conditioning system causes you to release less cold air out into your house. If your air filters are clogged, it will cause the coils to freeze and impede cold air even more! Sometimes a low quality or lousy fan or motor will not make your air conditioner unable to release cold air. Therefore, it is wise to call for an expert technician to tackle this issue.

Water leaks problem

A clogged condensate drain line might be one of the reasons behind the water leak problems inside your house. It causes the water to back up in the front and results in the overflow of water. If you ever find water pooling around the unit, it is a sign that the air filter is clogged. However, it can be fixed very easily to call an HVAC professional.
